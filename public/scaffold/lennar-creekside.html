Section 02 · The Campaign

A two-track campaign that compounds.

Conversion alone burns cold. Credibility alone doesn't move units. Together they make every paid post land on a buyer who already trusts the source. Tap each track to see the difference.

Track 01 · Paid by Lennar

Conversion Content

Floorplan tours, payment math, Fan and Carytown cost comparisons, garage value, model walkthroughs. Direct CTAs to the Welcome Home Center and weekend open houses.

  • "The Fan starts at 689K. Creekside Run starts at 344K. Same city, different math."
  • "Three floorplans, three buyers, three reasons to skip the inspection-heavy older home."
  • "11 minutes to Carytown when traffic behaves. That is the honest story."
  • "If your search keeps dying at inspection, look at the product where the systems are new from day one."
Track 02 · Organic on MAMS

Credibility Content

Forest Hill Avenue weekend loops, partner business features, the transparent "this is not Manchester" honesty post, James River access. Builds trust at the top of funnel before the hard ask.

  • "The Veil. Laura Lee's. Stella's. Cafe Zata. The daily Forest Hill life around Creekside Run."
